River Issues NZ

Our fresh waterways are degrading rapidly. Today, the impacts of pathogens, nutrient pollution, sedimentation, and changing flows are actively degrading New Zealand's rivers. Caused by human activity, these issues threaten human health, ruin our swimming areas, and contaminate drinking water. They affect ecosystems by suffocating riverbeds, triggering algal blooms, and destroying the food chain of our river ecosystems, causing fish to die. We will see more about the individual issues down below.
